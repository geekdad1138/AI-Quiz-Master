**Category**: school
**Level**: introductory
**Topics**: artificial intelligence, applications, machine learning, everyday uses

## About This Quiz

Fourth graders explore how Artificial Intelligence is used in the real world. This quiz covers practical applications of AI in daily life, entertainment, education, and more.
