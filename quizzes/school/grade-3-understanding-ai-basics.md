# Understanding AI Basics

**Category**: school
**Level**: introductory
**Topics**: artificial intelligence, learning, patterns, data
**Questions**: 8

---

## About This Quiz

Third graders are introduced to the concept of Artificial Intelligence (AI) - what it is, how it learns, and how it helps us. This quiz builds on their understanding of computers.
