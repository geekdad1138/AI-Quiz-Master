# Computers Around Us

**Category**: school
**Level**: introductory
**Topics**: computers, daily life, technology, basic understanding
**Questions**: 6

---

## About This Quiz

First graders will explore how computers are part of their everyday lives. This quiz introduces the idea that computers help us do many different things.

---

## Questions

### Question 1
**Type**: multiple-choice
**Difficulty**: easy

**Which of these has a computer inside it?**

- A) A microwave
- B) A toothbrush
- C) A refrigerator
- D) All of the above

---

### Question 2
**Type**: true-false
**Difficulty**: easy

**A computer can only do things that a person tells it to do.**

---

### Question 3
**Type**: multiple-choice
**Difficulty**: easy

**What do we call the person who tells a computer what to do?**

- A) A computer teacher
- B) A programmer or coder
- C) A computer doctor
- D) A robot maker

---

### Question 4
**Type**: true-false
**Difficulty**: easy

**The internet is a way for computers to talk to each other.**

---

### Question 5
**Type**: multiple-choice
**Difficulty**: easy

**What is a screen?**

- A) Something that keeps bugs out of a window
- B) A flat display that shows pictures and words from a computer
- C) A movie theater
- D) A piece of fabric

---

### Question 6
**Type**: true-false
**Difficulty**: easy

**Computers need electricity to work.**
