# AI Ethics and the Future

**Category**: school
**Level**: intermediate
**Topics**: artificial intelligence, ethics, responsibility, future implications
**Questions**: 10

---

## About This Quiz

Fifth graders explore important questions about AI ethics, responsibility, and the future. This quiz encourages critical thinking about the implications of artificial intelligence.

---

**Difficulty**: medium
