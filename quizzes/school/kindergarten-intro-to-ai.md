# Introduction to Computers and Robots

**Category**: school
**Level**: introductory
**Topics**: computers, robots, technology, basics
**Questions**: 5

---

## About This Quiz

This quiz introduces kindergarteners to basic concepts about computers and robots in a fun and accessible way. Students will learn simple vocabulary about technology in their daily lives.

---

## Questions

### Question 1
**Type**: true-false
**Difficulty**: easy

**A computer is a machine that can follow instructions.**

---

### Question 2
**Type**: multiple-choice
**Difficulty**: easy

**What is a robot?**

- A) A toy car
- B) A machine that can do tasks by itself
- C) A drawing
- D) A type of food

---

### Question 3
**Type**: true-false
**Difficulty**: easy

**Robots can think like people do.**

---

### Question 4
**Type**: multiple-choice
**Difficulty**: easy

**Which of these uses a computer?**

- A) A telephone
- B) A television
- C) An iPad
- D) All of the above

---

### Question 5
**Type**: true-false
**Difficulty**: easy

**You use a computer when you play video games.**

## Scoring Guide

- **100%**: Excellent! You're learning about technology!
- **80-99%**: Great job! You know a lot about computers!
- **60-79%**: Good try! Keep learning about robots and computers!
- **Below 60%**: That's okay! Technology is fun to learn about!
